# GoTriangle Partnership Pitch

EcoLoop proposes a **90-day recurring carpool pilot** in Research Triangle Park, operated as a **B2B2C program under GoTriangle / GoRTP** — complementing fixed-route transit and RTP Connect, not competing with them.

## One-sentence pitch

**EcoLoop fills the recurring daily commute gap for carless early-career workers in RTP — with measurable TDM outcomes GoTriangle can report, at lower per-trip cost than repeated TNC subsidies.**

## The problem GoTriangle already knows

- RTP is low-density; fixed-route service cannot reach every campus doorstep ([NC State ITRE microtransit report](https://itre.ncsu.edu/wp-content/uploads/2023/04/Microtransit-Report-3-8-23.pdf)).
- **RTP Connect** subsidizes Lyft/Uber ($10/trip) for trips touching the Regional Transit Center or Boxyard RTP ([rtpconnect.com](https://rtpconnect.com/)).
- That helps **one trip at a time** — not a **10-week summer intern** going to the same campus every weekday.
- Carless interns face $30–50/day Uber or 90-minute multi-leg transit; employers hear about it every summer.

## What EcoLoop adds

| RTP Connect / fixed route | EcoLoop |
|---------------------------|---------|
| One-off or first/last mile | **Recurring** Mon–Fri commute |
| Subsidized TNC or bus | **Matched carpool** on existing driver corridors |
| No relationship between trips | **Same driver, same rider** all season |
| Agency pays per TNC trip | **Flat $4–8/ride** P2P; optional program fee to EcoLoop |

## Pilot structure (proposed)

**Duration:** 90 days (align with summer intern season)  
**Geography:** Research Triangle Park corridors into major campuses (Cisco, IBM, TI, etc.)  
**Enrollment:** Co-branded link — `yoursite.com/join/gotriangle`  
**Reporting:** Live partner dashboard — `yoursite.com/partner/gotriangle`

### Success metrics (agree upfront)

1. **Enrollment:** 50+ onboarded commuters in pilot window
2. **Liquidity:** 15+ accepted recurring matches (rider–driver pairs)
3. **Utilization:** 70%+ ride completion rate for accepted matches
4. **Mode shift:** SOV trips avoided (completed carpools × 1)
5. **Environmental:** Miles shared, CO₂ avoided (EPA passenger-car equivalent)
6. **Subsidy comparison:** Estimated TNC subsidy $ avoided vs. baseline

### What GoTriangle provides

- Introduction to GoRTP employer network (intern-heavy campuses)
- Co-marketing to commuters (Share the Ride NC, GoPerks, employer TDM coordinators)
- Optional: small per-enrollment or per-completed-ride program fee (negotiate)
- Feedback on corridor priorities and transit stop handoffs

### What EcoLoop provides

- Matching platform (recurring schedule, zero-detour corridor algorithm)
- Verified commuter onboarding (.edu + employer email)
- Partner TDM dashboard with exportable metrics
- Pilot operations support and weekly metric reviews

## Why not Scoop?

[Scoop Commute](https://www.scoopcommute.com/) targets **1,000+ employee enterprises** with full enterprise contracts and Lyft backup. EcoLoop targets **seasonal intern cohorts** and **transit-agency pilots** — smaller, faster, cheaper to launch, aligned with GoTriangle's grant-funded TDM mission.

## Outreach path

### Step 1 — Warm intro (week 1)

Email **commute@rtp.org** (GoRTP / RTP transportation management):

> Subject: Pilot proposal — recurring carpools for carless RTP interns (complements RTP Connect)
>
> Short body: EcoLoop matches carless summer interns with drivers already on their corridor. We have a live pilot stack and TDM dashboard. Seeking 30-min call to explore a 90-day GoTriangle co-branded pilot. Deck + live demo links attached.

Attach: this doc, link to `/for-transit` and `/partner/gotriangle`.

### Step 2 — Employer pull (week 2–3)

Ask GoRTP for intro to **one** intern-heavy employer (Cisco RTP, IBM, TI). Offer:

- Free pilot enrollment for their 2026 intern cohort
- Weekly report: how many interns matched, rides completed

### Step 3 — Prove liquidity (week 4–8)

Goal: **10 real pairs** (not seed data). Without this, the pitch is slides only.

- On-site or virtual info session at one employer
- QR code → `/join/gotriangle`

### Step 4 — Review & expand (week 9–12)

Joint review with GoTriangle using `/partner/gotriangle` metrics. Decide: extend pilot, add employers, or apply for regional TDM grant funding.

## Revenue model (post-pilot)

- **Transit agency:** annual program license + per-active-commuter fee ($2–5/month)
- **Employer:** summer cohort package ($5K–15K) for branded enrollment + reporting
- **Grant-funded:** position as TDM mode-shift tool for regional sustainability grants

EcoLoop does **not** need to take a cut of Venmo fares in the pilot — prove value first, monetize the **program layer**.
